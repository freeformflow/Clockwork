Welcome to the ArcLight Scheduler web-interface. This app allows Human Resources to store the following information about a given crew member:
(1) Competencies (Pins)
(2) Standard Availability
(3) Special Availability Requests for Current Week

These data are used to staff schedule templates, while respecting turn-around time and total weekly work-hour constraints.

Schedule templates can be constructed automatically via user-set staffing guidelines. Each role can be named and given constraints as needed.
